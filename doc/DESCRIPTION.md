Ahwa convenes a small "council" of AI personas to deliberate on a
dilemma you bring to it. Each persona takes a turn around a virtual
table; a synthesizer reads the room and offers a unified take.

Designed for thinking out loud, not for chat. Use it for ethical
trade-offs, design decisions, life choices, debugging your own
arguments, anything where a one-shot LLM response would flatten
nuance.

**Privacy by design.** Self-hosted, single-user by default, no
analytics, no telemetry, no phone-home. Your dilemmas and the
council's responses live in a local SQLite file in your data
directory and go nowhere else.

**Bring your own models.** Vercel AI SDK under the hood; configure
any combination of Anthropic, OpenAI, OpenRouter, or a local Ollama
instance.

**Open source.** AGPL-3.0-or-later.
